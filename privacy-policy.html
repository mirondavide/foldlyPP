Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Foldly ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application that allows users to scan, store, and organize QR codes. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • QR code content that you scan or import
  • Card labels, colors, and folder organization preferences
  • App settings and preferences (e.g., onboarding status, favorites)

3. Camera Access

Foldly requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes. Camera data is processed in real-time on your device and is never transmitted, recorded, or stored beyond the scanned QR code content.

4. Photo Library Access

Foldly may request access to your photo library to allow you to import QR codes from saved images or save QR code images. We do not access, upload, or analyze any other photos in your library.

5. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard on-device storage mechanisms. We do not use cloud services, external databases, or remote servers to store your data. If you delete the app, all stored data is permanently removed.

6. Third-Party Services

Foldly does not integrate with analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools. We do not share any data with third parties.

The app may use the operating system's built-in share functionality and in-app review prompt, which are governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Foldly does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, the app is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
